- **Race:** Undead (Skeletal). **Class:** Skeletal Knight. **Background:** Noble Hawke family heir, cursed by **Lord Aldric Voss**.
- **Stats (4d6 drop lowest):** STR 15 (+2) sword arm; CHA 14 (+2) noble bearing; CON 13 (+1) undead resilience; WIS 12 (+1) fragmented instincts; INT 11 (+0) hazy memories; DEX 8 (−1) stiff skeletal joints.

- **Curse cost accumulating:** Day 2 of hard riding under open sun. Felt as "dullness" — not pain but something soft at the edges. The curse-binding absorbs what it can.

### Cover Identity — "The Hollow"

A being who underwent mystical transformation to achieve perfect clarity/purpose. Stripped mortal nature (food, sleep, comfort) to become focused. Represents the **Council of the Hollow** (a fictional secret org investigating magical/spiritual threats).

**VOWS:**

- Always wear the mask (a broken vow = broken spiritual discipline).
- Important discourse only OUTSIDE temples/cathedrals (spaces of balance between sacred/profane).
- Ascetic fasting (explains no eating).
- Meditation in place of sleep.

**MASK:** Custom **Kessler** design — serene carved leather, charcoal gray, full face, subtle raven symbol on the forehead, narrow eye holes.

**DOCUMENTS:** Forged letters of introduction from **Kessler**.

### Vulnerabilities

**Physical/Magical:** (1) Sunlight — prolonged exposure damages the curse-binding. (2) Holy water & blessed artifacts — pain/weaken the binding. (3) Decapitation/severe dismemberment — can destroy you. (4) Counter-magic — a skilled mage could unbind the curse. (5) The entity itself — extend consciousness too far and it could consume you.

**Behavioral Tells:** Doesn't eat/drink (cover: ritual fasting); doesn't sleep normally (cover: meditation); cold to the touch (cover: spiritual discipline); stiff movement when standing; **Aldric's** death could destabilize the binding.

## 🔮 SEAL & ENTITY DEEP LORE

**SEAL TRUE NATURE:** NOT built by humanity — created by the entity ITSELF. Voluntary self-imprisonment. The entity recognized its presence was incompatible with the emergence of human civilization. It created the seal from its own essence. It called the **Hawke** line to maintain it. **A partnership, not oppression.** Always meant to be TEMPORARY — a holding pattern. The question across centuries: "Are we ready to coexist?"

**ENTITY (the First Dreamer):** Pre-existed humanity. Not evil/good — fundamentally OTHER. Beyond a moral framework. Ancient, patient, conscious. Experiences our world as VOID/SILENCE where its presence should be. Wants ACKNOWLEDGMENT more than freedom. **Garrett Hawke** described it as sorrowful, lonely, NOT hostile. Currently pressing against the seal — could be a desire to escape OR a call for renegotiation.

**WHY WAKING NOW:** Jaycen broke his oath 3 years ago = fractured oath-magic. The seal has weakened exponentially since. It will fail catastrophically in ~4 months without intervention.
